The dungeon language, in one glance

name: / start: / objective:
The header. start: is the opening room; objective: reach <room> holding <item> is how you win. name: is flavour.
room <id> "<Name>"
A room. Indent the lines under it: plain prose is the description; items: a, b places takeable items.
exit <dir> -> <room>
A way out. One per line, indented under the room. exit north -> hall.
exit … requires item <x>
A gated exit. requires item lantern or requires flag door_open. Shown but barred until it holds.
use <item> on <target> in <room>
A use-rule. -> flag <name> "narration" sets a flag other gates read.
npc · hostile · combat · spell · light
Actors & systems. Trade with an NPC, fell a foe, learn a word of power, or race a burning lamp. See the Ember Observatory for every construct.

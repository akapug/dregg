Four small games over one idea: a story you can trust. An AI may narrate anything, but it can only do what the rules allow — prose is not power. A crowd can co-author a branch, and no one can quietly rewrite what was witnessed. A player can jailbreak the model and buy nothing. Every turn is a signed, replayable entry in a hash-chain a stranger can check.

Each turn: OPEN a vote over the party's candidate moves, the seated adventurers cast
write-once ballots, CLOSE it — and the winning move resolves through the SAME engine as
single-player. The vote is a plain MAJORITY tally (NOT a quorum certificate). The crowd
DECIDES; the world still RESOLVES — a party that votes a locked door is still refused.

The Overworld

one region · many dungeons · the map opens as you honestly clear it
The bundled adventures are not separate games — they are locations in one region joined by travel roads. A road into a deeper dungeon is sealed until you clear its prerequisite, and a location is only marked cleared when the service has re-verified a genuinely won chain for it. You cannot narrate, forge, or half-finish your way across the map: travel is verified-completion-gated.

What's real, and what's a first slice

  • Real: each dungeon stays independently verifiable — the same chain check every game ships. A location clears only on a re-verified Won chain (a forged, tampered, or unfinished session is refused), and a gated road opens only once its prerequisite is cleared.
  • First slice: progress is single-player and server-memory (it reflects the live game session). A fuller overworld persists progress per identity and folds each cleared dungeon's verified head into a region-level commitment.
  • The four registered dungeons open from the map; the venomous deep is wired into the region model but not yet in the play picker.

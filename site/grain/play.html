Boss an AI around a box it can't escape

Give an AI agent a job. Watch it work in a sealed sandbox — then check its work, and no one can lie to you about what it did.

The agent runs inside a rented sandbox with a fixed budget and a short list of things it's allowed to touch. It can propose anything. The box only lets through what you permitted.

Step 1Bring a key for the AI

The thinking happens at an AI provider. Paste an OpenRouter key — the free tier works fine.

🔐 It stays in your browser. Your key is sent only to OpenRouter to do the thinking — never to the sandbox. The sandbox only ever sees the single action the AI wants to take.
Why do I need this?

The AI's "brain" is a language model, and running it costs a little money — so it runs under your account, not ours. That's also what keeps your key private: it never leaves this page except to talk to the model provider. A free OpenRouter key is plenty for this demo.

Step 2Tell your agent what to do

Type a job, or tap an example. The sandbox is allowed to work with files in its own scratch folder — nothing else.

budget: 0 of 100,000 spent

Step 3Check the agent's work

You don't have to take the sandbox's word for it. Re-derive the record yourself — the sandbox can't have faked a single step.

Public proof record 06 / DrEX

The Dragon’s Exchange

A fair place to trade.

Orders stay sealed until the batch closes. Every trade in that batch clears at one price. The result carries a proof.

Present scope: proven market machinery and a running developer instance—not a public real-money exchange.

01

The ordinary market

Someone in the middle gets to choose.

A normal exchange sees orders before they are final. Whoever controls the order of events can put a trade first, move another back, or let a bot race ahead.

01

Bots jump the line

They see an order, buy ahead of it, and sell back a heartbeat later.

02

The order shapes the price

The person deciding what happens first holds power over the result.

03

The operator remains trusted

An audit or safety badge does not remove the operator’s ability to fail or cheat.

02

The mechanism

The sealed envelope.

  1. 1

    Seal

    Each trader writes an order into an envelope nobody else can read in time to act on it.

  2. 2

    Close

    The short order window ends. There is no first order to reward and no last order to punish.

  3. 3

    Reveal

    Every envelope opens together. No one had a moment to reorder the batch for gain.

  4. 4

    Clear

    Every matching trade settles at one price, all at once or not at all.

Economists call this a sealed-bid batch auction. DrEX adds a result that does not require trusting the person who ran it.

03

The receipt

The machine checks the whole batch.

When a batch clears, DrEX produces a proof: a piece of reasoning a machine can re-check step by step. It covers the settlement rules, not a sample trade.

  • Money went in and out in equal measure.
  • No buyer paid more than offered.
  • No seller received less than asked.
  • The batch happened together, or not at all.

If one statement is false, the proof does not check. Anyone can run that check on their own machine.

04

What follows

The rules bind the builder, too.

Private until clearing

No operator watches the order.

Your order stays yours until the batch clears. The resulting trade is then settled in the open for anyone to re-check.

One rule for everyone

No place at the front.

A bot, operator, or builder cannot hand itself a better price or ordering advantage.

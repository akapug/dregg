deos-chat

The deos Matrix chat — the social layer over the dregg world. Rooms are cells; a message can CARRY A REHYDRATABLE MEMBRANE (a cap-bounded fork of the world the recipient drives live, then stitches back fail-closed via branch-and-stitch). Reactions, replies, edits/redactions-as-state, person-trust badges. Built on matrix-rust-sdk + gpui-component.

inspector-card

THE REFLECTIVE TURN (rung 2): the Inspect-mode main surface reborn as a live deos-js CARD. The focused cell's moldable faces are generated into a `CardPane` entity over the LIVE `World`; a face's affordance button fires a REAL verified turn on the operator's cell and the card re-reads it. The surface that inspects the image is itself a card the image renders — the frame becoming yours to reshape from within. LAZY: built on first paint, rebuilt when focus moves; `#[cfg(all(feature = "dev-surfaces", feature = "card-pane"))]` (`cockpit/mod.rs::InspectorCardMount`, `card_pane.rs`). Baked via the `inspector` tab (the card IS the Inspect surface when card-pane is on).

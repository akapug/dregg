webshell

A general http(s):// browser surface (distinct from web-of-cells): a real gpui-component URL bar (Enter-to-go), back/forward/reload, and a content tile rendered through the Servo SWGL pipeline behind the net-cap allowlist; fail-closed (a cap refusal shows in-band, the tile never silently blanks).

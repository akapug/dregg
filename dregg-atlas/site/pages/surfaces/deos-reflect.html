The deos-reflect SUBSTRATE is the gpui-free, cap-bounded reflective core extracted as its own normal workspace member — the shared shape that both the dregg-mcp server and the JS scripting env bind to. It is pure functions of a bare dregg_cell::Ledger plus its receipts: no gpui, no cockpit World, no parallel model. This is what lets reflection be reused off the raw substance and de-bloats the eventual cockpit.

What it provides (five modules): substance — the four substances plus Inspectable, reading fields PUBLICLY so a Committed field redacts to its commitment, never the value. graph — OcapGraph over the ledger: nodes, edges, reachability, delegation layers, cycles. frustum — the per-viewer cap-bounded crawl, where an unreachable cell is absent rather than forged (the anti-substitution shape). affordances — cap-gated message projection by is_attenuation, decoupled from any window cap onto bare AuthRequired (a weaker viewer sees fewer affordances, an admin more; lacking authority is refused). present — the substrate-pure moldable faces: RawFields · Graph · DomainVisual · Provenance.

What it proves: the algorithms here are ported from starbridge-v2's gpui-free reflect/graph/affordance layers, rebased off the cockpit World onto the bare substance — so the SAME reflective guarantees (public-field redaction, cap-bounded crawl, attenuation-gated affordances) hold on a plain Ledger, independent of the desktop. Reproduce: cd deos-reflect && cargo test (5/5). It is the substrate under both the scripting JS surface above and the dregg-mcp driving harness.
